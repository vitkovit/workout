Phase 1: RE-ENTRY

Weeks 1–4 | ~12 sessions | Rebuild work capacity & motor patterns

Phase Goals & Guidelines

Volume: 3 sets × 12–15 reps per exercise | 5–6 exercises per session

Duration: ~60 minutes per session

Cardio: 20 min low-intensity (walking/incline treadmill) post-session

Machines & light barbells favored

Phase 3: HYPERTROPHY + CUT

Weeks 13–28 | ~48 sessions | Maximum hypertrophy with progressive deficit

Phase Goals & Guidelines

Primary Objective: Maximize hypertrophy stimulus while managing progressive caloric deficit. Rotate exercises every 4 weeks for novelty stimulus.

Training Focus: High exercise diversity, 7–8 exercises per session, varied equipment (cables, machines, dumbbells, barbells)

Intensity: RPE 8–9 (progressive overload)

Volume: 4 sets × 8–12 reps (main), 3 sets × 12–15 reps (isolation) | Total 7–8 exercises per session

Duration: ~85–95 minutes per session

Cardio: 30–40 min moderate, increasing frequency (2–3× weekly)

Exercise Rotation: Change 40–50% of exercises every 4 weeks within this phase

Phase 3A (Weeks 13–20)
Moderate deficit, ~2,500 kcal
Phase 3B (Weeks 21–28)
Deeper deficit, ~2,200 kcal
Rest Between Sets
65–90 seconds (brief for hypertrophy)

Phase 4: CONTEST PREP

Weeks 29–40+ | 12+ sessions | Maintain muscle, strip fat, peak conditioning

Phase Goals & Guidelines

Primary Objective: Maintain hard-earned muscle while achieving stage-ready conditioning. Manage fatigue and central nervous system (CNS) load on aggressive deficit.

Training Focus: More machine & isolation work to reduce joint stress on low calories. Maintain intensity but slightly reduce volume.

Intensity: RPE 8–8.5 (maintain load, reduce reps/volume)

Volume: 3–4 sets × 6–10 reps (compounds), 3 sets × 12–15 reps (isolation) | 6–7 exercises per session

Duration: ~75–85 minutes per session

Cardio: 40–60 min (mix LISS + brief HIIT)

Critical Note: Recommend working with a prep coach during final 8 weeks for conditioning assessment, posing practice, and supplementation guidance

Caloric Deficit
~2,000 kcal (aggressive)
Training Priority
Maintain load, reduce volume slightly
Recovery Tools
Sleep, massage, deload management
